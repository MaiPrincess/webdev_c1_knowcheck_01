About Me

¡Hola! My name is Theresa Rice, and I am a passionate Mexican woman with a heart full of creativity and a love for traditional crafts. Allow me to share a little about myself and my many interests!

Cooking and Baking

Cooking and baking have always been a significant part of my life. Drawing inspiration from the rich culinary heritage of Mexico, I delight in preparing authentic dishes bursting with flavor and color. From savory tamales to indulgent tres leches cake, I pour my love into every recipe, infusing each dish with the warmth and spice of my cultural roots.

Sewing and Crafting

With nimble fingers and a keen eye for detail, I find joy in the art of sewing and crafting. Whether it's stitching vibrant patterns into traditional Mexican textiles or fashioning intricate designs onto handmade garments, I take pride in preserving and celebrating our cultural heritage through the textile arts.

Handcrafted Soaps, Candles, and Wax Melts

As a maker of cold-processed soaps, candles, and wax melts, I embrace the beauty of natural ingredients and the artistry of scent. Each handcrafted creation is a labor of love, carefully curated to evoke memories, soothe the senses, and bring a touch of luxury to everyday life.

Nourishing Body Butter

Drawing inspiration from the bountiful gifts of nature, I craft nourishing body butter infused with indigenous botanicals and traditional remedies. These decadent formulations are designed to pamper the skin, leaving it soft, supple, and radiant with health.

Photography

Capturing the beauty of the world through the lens of my camera is another passion of mine. Whether it's documenting the vibrant colors of a bustling mercado or the serene landscapes of the Mexican countryside, I seek to preserve moments of beauty and joy in every photograph.

In all that I do, I am fueled by a deep appreciation for my Mexican heritage and a desire to share its richness with the world. Join me on this journey as I continue to explore, create, and celebrate the vibrant tapestry of Mexican culture!
